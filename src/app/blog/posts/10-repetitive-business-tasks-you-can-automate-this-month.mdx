You do not need to automate everything. These ten tasks cover common small-business pain points.

## The list

1. **Meeting notes to tasks** — paste notes, get action items, review and add to your list
2. **Routine email drafts** — prompts for scheduling, follow-ups, and status updates
3. **Lead intake summary** — new inquiry → CRM record → AI summary of what they need
4. **Weekly planning** — Friday review: wins, blockers, next week priorities
5. **Content repurposing** — one idea → email snippets and social variants
6. **SOP drafting** — rough notes → structured process doc draft
7. **Customer FAQ responses** — prompt library for your top support questions
8. **Proposal outlines** — first draft from bullet points and scope notes
9. **Invoice follow-up reminders** — task triggers, not auto-send without review
10. **Onboarding checklists** — consistent steps for every new client

## Start with one

Pick the task that would save you the most time this week. Run it for two weeks before adding the next.
