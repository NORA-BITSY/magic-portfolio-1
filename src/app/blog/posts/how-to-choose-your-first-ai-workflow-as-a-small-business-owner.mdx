Most small businesses do not need a complex AI stack. They need one reliable workflow that reduces repeated work.

## Start with the task, not the tool

Pick a task that is repeatable, low-risk, and time-heavy. Good first candidates: email drafts, meeting summaries, lead follow-up reminders, and weekly planning.

## A simple decision filter

Automate tasks that meet all three:

1. **Repeatable** — happens at least weekly with similar steps
2. **Low-risk** — a human can review output before it goes anywhere important
3. **Time-heavy** — takes more than 15 minutes each time

## What to leave alone (for now)

High-stakes client decisions, one-off creative strategy, and workflows your team has not documented yet.
