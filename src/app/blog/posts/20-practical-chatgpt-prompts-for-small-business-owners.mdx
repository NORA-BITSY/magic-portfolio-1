Generic prompts produce generic answers. Business prompts need context, constraints, and a clear output format.

## Prompt framework

1. **Role** — who the AI should act as
2. **Context** — your business, audience, and situation
3. **Task** — what you need done
4. **Constraints** — tone, length, what to avoid
5. **Output format** — bullets, email, table, checklist

## Example categories

- **Email:** follow-ups, scheduling, thank-yous, status updates
- **Operations:** SOP drafts, meeting summaries, task extraction
- **Marketing:** social variants, newsletter outlines, headline options
- **Sales:** lead summaries, qualification notes, proposal outlines
- **Support:** FAQ answers, response drafts, escalation summaries

Save prompts that work. Reuse and improve them — that is how you build systems.
