Leads go cold fast. But automated follow-up that sounds robotic can hurt your brand more than slow follow-up.

## The balance

Automate **capture, reminders, and drafts**. Keep **personalization and sending** human-reviewed.

## A simple system

1. **Capture** — one intake form or unified inbox for all lead sources
2. **Record** — CRM entry with source, date, and notes
3. **Summarize** — AI summary and suggested next steps
4. **Draft** — follow-up email draft based on the inquiry
5. **Remind** — task if no reply in 48 hours
6. **Review** — human reads and sends every outbound message

## What this replaces

Checking multiple inboxes, forgetting follow-ups after busy days, and rewriting similar "thanks for reaching out" emails.
